Xinyan Zhao

Xinyan is a Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Huanhuan Chen in School of Data Science, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). Her current research interest is knowledge based dialogue systems.

Previously, Xinyan received her bachelor's degrees in civil engineering from Hohai University (HHU).
